Why Unbound Force?

An AI agent swarm that earns its place through structure, quality, and transparency.

{{/* Featured card — full width */}}

Spec to Demo in One Command

Run /unleash and the swarm handles clarification, planning, implementation, testing, and review autonomously. It exits when it needs your judgment and resumes where it left off. No manual step-by-step orchestration required.

{{/* Supporting cards — 3 columns */}}

A Team That Knows Your Codebase

Five specialized AI personas — Product Owner, Developer, Tester, Reviewer Council, and Manager — each with domain expertise and access to your project's full context through semantic search.

Every Change Tested and Reviewed

Quality gates at every stage. Gaze detects untested side effects, the Divisor review council validates from five perspectives, and nothing merges without passing CI and code review.

Open Source

Fully open source under Apache 2.0 license. Contribute, customize, and build with the community.
